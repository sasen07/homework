Ramesh Manang

Ramesh has been tattooing since 2000. Throughout his career he has worked in various tattoo studios, won awards at tattoo conventions and worked guest spots alongside some of the best tattoo artists in the world, learning from the best and perfecting his technique along the way. With a killer combination of many years of experience and an excellent understanding of colour theory. In 2014 he decided it was time to start his own tattoo studio and opened Tattoos Nepal.

Suzal Tandukar

Suzal is an all round artist. He has been tattooing since 2002, and has an eye for the finer details, this really shows in his black and grey wildlife portraits, which he specialises in. Suzal also enjoys geometric, dot-work and blackwork styles which he draws directly onto the skin to give them a unique flow.

Lisa Thapa

After finishing her degree in illustration it wasn’t long before Lisa started her career as a tattoo artist back in 2011, with such an intriguing, unique style of work that has not gone unnoticed, Lisa has been featured in several tattoo magazines and blogs all over the world.
She finds her inspiration from the more traditional style of tattooing with an illustrative twist, using the contrast of bold line work with a bright colour pallet, Lisa can transform almost any everyday object into a fun, desirable piece of artwork.

Bidur Dhakal

Bidur has been tattooing since 2010 specialising in all forms of black work, geometry, Polynesian, Thai, Borneo/Dayak and Marquesan. Bidur is a multi award winning artist with a love of large scale work so he can fully express his innovative ideas. He has completed and is currently working on a number of exceptional body suits however is more than happy to take on smaller work too.

Manoj K.C.

Manoj has tattooing experience of 8 years. He does almost all forms but he loves color tattoos and portraits to give to the clients. Manoj also enjoys black and grey, and, dot-work designs.
