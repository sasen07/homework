About Tattoos Nepal

Established in 2014 A.D. in heart of our capital, Thamel, Tattoos Nepal has been providing tattooing and piercing services. Tattoos Nepal exceeds tattoo industry standards in quality and hygiene. We use almost advanced medical field sterilization techniques.


We specialize in creating custom pieces. but, if you prefer, we have thousands of designs for you to choose from. We also offer tattoo re design and cover ups and piercings as well.


Tattoos Nepal heartly welcomes you to our studio to get your dream tattoo and have an amazing experience.
